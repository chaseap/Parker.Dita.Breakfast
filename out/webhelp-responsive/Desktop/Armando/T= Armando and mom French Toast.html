French Toast

Here's a recipe that will work for both Armando and Mom! All you need to do is switch out the milks and you're good!

Below are the ingredients and steps that will tell you how to make vegan French toast.
Ingredients:
  • 6 slices of bread, sliced about ¾ inch thick
  • 1 cup Milk
  • Use almond milk (Armando)
  • Use whole milk (mom)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup, plus more for serving
  • 2 tablespoons millet flour
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
  • Tiny pinch of salt
  • Coconut oil for the pan
  • Any toppings you want (sugar, fruites, syrup, etc.)
    Steps:
    1. In a small bowl, whisk milk, maple syrup, flour, nutritional yeast, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt together.
    2. Place the bread in a shallow dish (with sides) that holds all of the bread. Pour mixture over the bread, then lift or flip the bread over to make sure both sides are coated.
    3. Heat a drizzle of coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat and add the bread once the bread is hot. Once one side gets golden, flip the bread and cook both sides evenly.
    4. Take bread off of the pan when both sides are golden-brown and serve with any topping you'd like.
