Blueberry Oatmeal Waffles

This vegan waffle recipe will have your taste buds screaming "thank you!"

Below are the ingredients and instructions for how to make this delicious breakfast food.
Note: You will need a waffle iron
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup white whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 cup quick cooking oats
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 3 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups frozen blueberries
    Steps:
    1. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and allspice into mixing bowl.
    2. Mix in oats
    3. Make well in the center and add applesauce, milk, maple syrup, oil and vanilla
    4. Stir until combined
    5. Let batter rest for five minutes, then fold in blueberries
    6. Cook in waffle iron according to manufacturer directions
