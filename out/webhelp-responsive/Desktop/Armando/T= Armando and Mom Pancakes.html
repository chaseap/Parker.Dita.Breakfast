Lemon Pancake

Don't let the lemon fool you. These pancakes aren't sour, but they are mighty tasty! This will also work for both Armando and Mom! All you need to do is use either mashed bananas or eggs, respectively!

Below are the ingredients and steps that tell you how to make lemon vegan pancakes.
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup vanilla, soy, or almond milk
  • 1 cup and 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice with ½ tablespoon of fresh zest
  • 1/3 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ tablespoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • Eggs (for Mom)
  • Mashed banana (for Armando)
    Steps:
    1. Add dry ingredients into a tall bowl and stir them all together.
    2. Add wet ingredients and fold well.
    3. *For mom: Add eggs with lemon juice and zest. Then Whip all ingredients together.
    4. *For Armando: Add mashed banana and lemon juice and zest. Then whip all ingredients together.
    5. Put burner on low as you warm your skillet or frying pan. You will want the pan to be evenly hot before you start to cook the pancake.
    6. Add a small splash of oil to your pan and distribute it around the pan evenly. Then add ½ cup or so of batter and allow it to spread in the pan. Put the burner on medium heat setting.
    7. Allow pancake to slowly cook, and flip the pancake once the edges begin to form and the batter is fluffly – not wet.
    8. Once both sides are a brownish-golden color, you can take the pancakes off of the pan and serve them on a plate!
